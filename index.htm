Welcome to Powering Maths !

Education of children is the top most priority of every parent. Nurturing one’s child with a liberal dose of education is every parent’s aspiration. That education is one investment which is recession-proof is an oft proven fact.

The seed of education is sown with the interaction of a child with the ecosystem he grows in. The more formal education set up forms the motive force with the commencement of graded/school education. Without having any choice of choosing one’s subject during the formative schooling years, child is burdened with a vast array of subjects. Among all the subjects taught at school, Maths undoubtedly takes the cake for being the toughest nut to crack - A MYTH ALSO BEING PROPAGATED BY MANY & NEEDS TO BE BURIED FOR GOOD. This is precisely what POWeRING mATHS aims for – to demystify maths for students by generating interest & love for this greatly misunderstood subject.

The seed of POWERING MATHS was sown a couple of years ago. The journey from concept to incubation to execution was along only to ensure a distinctly better & compelling alternative to students desirous of improving their proficiency in maths. The centre was opened in 2011 with a very single-minded aim of building an exemplary breed of students (from Class VI to Class XI) who are enchanted by the challenges posed by maths & take pride in demolishing the myth of formidable tag attached to this subject.
